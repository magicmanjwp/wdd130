Site Name

Game Night

Purpose

Have you wanted to have a fun game night but didn’t know what games to play? I love playing games with family and friends and will help you know what games would be best to have at your game night. I will also provide a description of the game including the best amount of people to play. I will also provide the best place to buy the game from, so you don’t need to travel to thousands of game shops and websites.

Why visit our site and what to expect

You will want to visit my site so you can learn more about the games that you can play the next time your family gets together for some fun. To help you decide if a game is fit for you, we will provide a short description of what a game so you can know which games you would want to learn. If you don’t have time to read the descriptions provided, we will also provide a video of a play through so that you can see and hear what the game is like. Not only that, but it will also be a quick and easy way to learn how to play the game while some more experienced people play the game and talk it out, step by step.

We will make sure that the site is clear on the different types of games. We will split the game into three different categories. Those categories are first, card games; second, board games; and third, other games. Card games will cover a wide variety of games from easy games like Go Fish and Uno to more complicated games like Mafia and Tichu. Board games will cover any games that have a board aspect to the game. These games include Monopoly, Life, and even Secret Hitler. The ‘other games’ categories is vague because it will include games that you can play online and games that don’t need cards or boards. These types of games include Among Us, Cubb, Bocce Ball, and even Croquet. As we find and receive different games to add to the collection, we will add more sections to make it easier for you to find the game that you are looking for.

What questions can be answered through our site

We will be able to help you know the number of players recommended by the game and how many people we recommend for the best game play. We will also provide a simple to understand version of the rules so you can learn and play the game as quickly as possible. Another thing that we will do is help you find where you can buy the game for the best price. We know that there are some games that you wish you could play but you need more people than you have so what can you do? Some games cannot change how many people you need to play but we will find different ways to play games to accommodate the lack of personnel.

Why our site instead of another

We will provide a wide variety of games from card games and board games to electronic and out-door games. Whether you have a group of younger, new game players or a clan of seasoned players, we have the games that are right for you. If you have been playing basic games for ever and want to change up how you play, we will find variations that you can play that other websites will not provide. Other websites will only give you information on a few games. However, if there is a game that you want to learn about and we don’t have it on our page, let us know and we will find the information that you seek. This is not just a website that the Game Night team creates, it is a website that the gaming community creates. If you know of different rules for a game that you like to play, you can also let us know of such rules so that we can provide as many variations as possible.
