Game Night

Game Type

Outdoor Games

Is the weater nice out today? Are you tired of sitting on the couch looking a different screens? Outdoor games are perfect for nice sunny days or warm nights. There are a lot of different types of games that you can play out side if you have the space. These games include but are not limited to: Tag, Dodge Ball, Basket Ball, Frisbee, Kubb, and many more.

Card/Board Games

So you want to play some fun games with your friends and family but it is raining outside. What are you supposed to do now that you are stuck inside? Board and card games are excellent games to play if you are stuck inside or simpliy don't want to go outside. The different possibilities for games to choose are endless. A few Game Night favorites include but are not limited to: Tichu, One Night Werewolf, Salem, Secert Hittler, Tsuro, and many more.

Online Games

Imagine you want to have a game night with your friends but they moved to a different state. How are you going to have a game night with them now? Luckily for you, there are still games that you can play with people that aren't able to be with you in person. Online games are a great way to stay connected to your friends and family when your situation prohibits you from meeting together. Some games that can keep you together include but are not limited to: Halo, Minecraft, Mario Kart Tour, JackBox Games, Among Us, and many more.

Improv Games

Do you want to have fun but don't necessarily want to do as much as the other people in your group? If so, improv games are the games for you. Improv games are games that are generally played by actors to help them come up with random things to do and say on the spot. However, you don't need to be an actor to play the games or to enjoy watching others play. Most of these games are best played when there are at least four people playing the game and another group of at least 3 people watching. Good examples of these games include but are not limited to: Lie Detector, Freeze Frame, Late Again, I Can't Do That, Gibberish Murder, and many more.
