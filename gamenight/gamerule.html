How to Play

Kubb - (Outdoor)

Kubb (pronounced 'Koob') is a fun game where two teams of 1-6 players try to knock-down the opponents kubbs in an attempt to knock-over the king and win the game. You don't want to knock-over the king, however, before your opponents loses all their kubbs or you lose the game.

You can buy this game for $34.99 on Amazon.

YouTube Description

Tsuro - (Car/Board)

Tsuro (pronounced Suro) is a path building game in which you are trying to have your piece the last remaining piece on the board. However, you must be careful with the path you choose because your opponents are trying to kick you off the board. Kick them off first and you will be one step closer to winning.

You can buy this game for $39.95 on Amazon.

YouTube Description

Among Us - (Online)

Among us is a popular social deduction video game in which all the crewmates are on a mission to complete all of their tasks. However, there is an imposter among them that is trying to kill everyone before all the tasks are completed. Can you find the imposter and win the game, or will he find you first?

You can get this game for free on your phone or for $4.99 on the Microsoft Store.

YouTube Description

Gibberish Murder - (Improv)

This game is a classic improv game in which the people participating have lost the power of speech and can now only talk in gibberish. One of the participents is told by those watching who died in a murder, where the murder takes place, and what object was used.

You don't need to buy anything for this game.
